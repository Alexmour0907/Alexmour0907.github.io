About Me

I'm Alexander Mouritsen, a 16 year old junior fullstack web developer from Bergen, Norway. I've always been curious about technology, and over time that interest naturally led me into coding. Right now, I'm in my first year at Amalie Skram VGS, where I'm continuing to explore and learn more.

I work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the front-end, and use Node.js and Express for back-end development. I also use SQLite for managing databases and building full web applications that handle data efficiently.

When I build something, I try to keep the code clean and the user experience smooth. I like turning ideas into working websites or apps whether it's something simple or more advanced.

I'm open for freelance work alongside school and especially enjoy working with small businesses or individuals who want a website that feels unique.
