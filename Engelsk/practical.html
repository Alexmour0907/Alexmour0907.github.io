Practical information


This information may be useful when visiting Singapore.



Weather and time zone

Singapore's tropical climate (25-33C) gives you the opportunity to enjoy summer all year long.
Dress accordingly, choose light clothing but be aware that showers might occur - bring an umbrella. Singapore uses the GMT +8 time zone.

Language

Singapore does have four official languages, and one of them is English. You should be perfectly fine with English, but you may also hear people talk mandarin.

Singapore visitor Centers


If you need any assistance or have any questions, you can swing by any of the visitor centers.
You will be able to get tips on recommended visiting points across the entire Country.


Particularly strict environmental regulations


Singapore takes its environmental laws seriously. Littering is more than only frowned-upon, it can lead to hefty fines or even jail time.
So, remember to keep an eye out for trash bins and make sure to dispose of your waste properly.
