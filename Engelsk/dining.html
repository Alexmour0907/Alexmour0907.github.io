Top dining experiences


Synthesis restaurant


The synthesis restaurant is a local restaurant in the Marina Bay area.
It’s a restaurant serving delicious Chinese food and with its both fancy and cozy inside,
it’s a perfect place to eat some dinner. But beware that there is a dress code for this place,
“Smart, dress to impress” is the quoted dress code. For the menu and more information,
you can visit their website here.


Ocean restaurant


Have you ever wanted to eat dinner along with the sea life? Then the
Ocean restaurant might be the perfect place for you.
Here you get to dine alongside manta rays, reef sharks and a whole
bunch of tropical fish. The Ocean restaurant also has a dress code.
It is encouraged to dress smart and elegantly.
For more information on this, please visit their website here.


Breakfast with the wildlife

If you decide to visit the zoo during your potential trip to Singapore,
breakfast with the wildlife is something almost impossible to resist.
You get to come close whit the wildlife when you eat your breakfast.
Please note that you need an admission ticket to the Singapore zoo.
For the price and more details about the breakfast you can visit their website here.
